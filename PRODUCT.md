# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local service businesses such as mobile detailers, PDR companies, contractors, roofers, movers, landscapers, and other appointment-based businesses.

## Product Purpose
Generate qualified strategy calls and lead-form submissions for local service businesses.

## Brand Personality
Aggressive, dark, premium "bro marketing" feel. A combination of a performance marketing agency, automotive brand, and modern software company.

## Anti-references
- Purple colors
- Cartoonish graphics
- Saturated AI cream/sand/beige default background
- Generic bright SaaS gradients

## Design Principles
- More bookings, less guesswork.
- Bulletproof high-converting structure.
- Premium dark aesthetics with aggressive red highlights.
- Clear visual hierarchy with zero placeholder text.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA accessibility contrast.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
- Fully keyboard-accessible navigation and interactive process controls.
